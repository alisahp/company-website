About Us

Brief Introduction.

MyBestSea-Ali-SaIT-DevOp-Engineer is a U.S.-based, global business technology consultancy with offices in Chicago ,IL. We specialize in building and implementing managed services and solutions for customers looking to digitally transform their business, whether they are at the beginning or further along their digital transformation journey.

Our team of IT professionals possess significant expertise in the areas of Cloud Migration and Automation, Internet of Things, Big Data optimization. They apply their business and technical knowledge to help clients enhance their existing work processes and infrastructure in order to secure new efficiencies, boost productivity and increase company-wide cost-effectiveness. Given the current, ever-evolving digital landscape, Techolution delivers to customers both tangible business value in the present and in the future.

Our Mission

We founded MyBestSea-Ali-SaIT-DevOp-Engineer with one mission- to help businesses increase productivity and profitability by making IT a streamlined part of operations.
